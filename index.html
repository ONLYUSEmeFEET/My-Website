SEGA Chill Editions

Modding classic SEGA games to be more accessible!

When SEGA updated SEGA Mega Drive and Genesis Classics in 2016, they added Steam Workshop support. I began my series of "Chill Edition" mods of classic SEGA games and now have over 50,000 downloads total.

So what is a Chill Edition mod? Chill Edition mods add additional features, enabling people to experience games at their own pace. Individual features vary by game, but some features I frequently add to games are infinite lives and level select. These features make classic SEGA games possible to beat for people that normally could not see the end of the game. They also allow people that can beat these SEGA games to play through them again at a relaxed pace!

Some of my most popular Chill Edition mods include: Streets of Rage 2, Gunstar Heroes, and Ranger of Rage 2.

GalactiMAX

Fly through the galaxy and pierce the heavens!

Initially created for a game jam in 12 hours, GalactiMAX is all about flying though the galaxy killing aliens to power up your spaceship! The theme of the jam was "Connect" so as you progress through the game, your ship combines with other ships to power up. The game is inspired by early space arcade games as well the anime Tengen Toppa Gurren Lagann.

I was the game designer and programmer in addition to coming up with the game's concept. I began making GalactiMAX a Galaga clone when I realized the game would work better as a scrolling shooter like Xevious. I also decided to gradually turn the game into a bullet-hell shooter as the game progressed to make the player feel more powerful.

The game is also inclusive by design. The ship fires automatically at enemies so the only movement input is needed. In addition, the game has high-contrast graphics and is colorblind-friendly by shaping enemy bullets different from the player's bullets.

Unreal Editor: Hell's Arena

Rocket Jump from Hell!

This mod was made for the Epic Games' latest Unreal Tournament game using the Unreal Editor.

Hell's Arena is a deathmatch map for up to 4 players focused on crazy action and quick map traversal. The cross in the center of the map contains powerful items however, it will cause damage to the player. Mastering a variety of weapons is key to victory as is controlling the center of the map. Usage of jump pads is encouraged and can get you around the map quickly!

    Protect the Rocket!

    Let's Rocket!

    Protect your rocket! Tap to defeat enemy ships. The sooner you tap, the more points you earn. But be careful! You have to hold your fire and save friendly pink aliens. Destroy a pink alien or fail to defeat an enemy ship and it's game over!

    Speech synthesis is enabled by default to read out information for low vision players. Simply tap the top-right speech pause button to disable this feature. An additional game speed feature is also included for accessibility, to allow players to play at their own pace. Made during Train Jam 2017 with the theme "Unexpected Anticipation."

    Arcade Hysteria

    Escape the madness!

    Built in 72 hours for the Indies vs Gamers game jam, Arcade Hysteria traps you in an arcade machine trying to keep your sanity. While initially appearing like a normal endless runner game, let your sanity below 50% and witness the world in hysteria! I was the game designer and a programmer.

    Since Arcade Hysteria is an endless runner game, I wanted to innovate somehow to make it unique. I chose to implement the sanity meter as well as the two worlds the player traverses. The dual world concept was inspired by the Legacy of Kain series. I also wanted the music to change with the game's environment so I implemented dynamic audio.

    The game can be played with one switch; any button will both start the game and make the player jump. The visuals also change shape along with color during sanity transitions to be colorblind-friendly. And while it's cool, the dynamic audio is not necessary to enjoy the game.
